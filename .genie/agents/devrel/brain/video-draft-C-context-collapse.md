# Video Draft C: "Context Collapse"

## Concept
Visual storytelling. No words needed. Show the PROBLEM — 7 chaotic agent tabs, copy-pasting, merge conflicts, confusion. Then the SOLUTION — one command, orchestrated harmony. Music does all the emotional work. Think the transition from chaos to order in a Shell F1 ad.

TONE: Cinematic. Problem → solution arc. Dissonance → harmony.
AUDIENCE: Developers who already use multiple AI agents and feel the pain

## Why This Works
- Every developer using AI agents HAS felt this pain — instant recognition
- The chaos → order transition is inherently satisfying (ASMR for devs)
- No words needed — the visual contrast tells the whole story
- The 0.5s silence before the resolution creates a dopamine hit
- The music carries the emotional weight — orchestral = epic, important

## Risk
- Requires good "chaos" footage — needs to feel authentic, not staged
- More production effort than text-based concepts
- Mitigation: use REAL recordings of multi-agent sessions — authenticity is the whole point
