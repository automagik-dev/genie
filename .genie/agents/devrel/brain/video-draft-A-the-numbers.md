# Video Draft A: "The Numbers"

## Concept
Pure data shock. No explanation. Let developers do the math. Bold typography over dark terminal aesthetics. The numbers are so absurd they demand a rewatch.

## Why This Works
- Numbers are universal scroll-stoppers
- Developers are data-driven — they'll verify these claims (which drives repo visits)
- Each number is more absurd than the last — escalating disbelief
- No explanation needed — respect the audience's intelligence
- The silence (no voiceover) makes it feel like a statement, not a pitch

## Risk
- Could feel like vanity metrics without context
- Mitigation: the screen recordings behind the text SHOW the real work happening
