# Video Draft D: "It Ships Itself"

## Concept
Meta-proof. The most powerful claim in software: dogfooding at the extreme. Genie doesn't just help OTHER people ship — it ships ITSELF. 96% of its own PRs pass review on the first try. The code writes the code. Recursive confidence.

## Why This Works
- Self-referential proof is the strongest marketing claim possible
- Developers LOVE meta (see: compilers that compile themselves, Rust bootstrapping)
- "Open source. MIT license." removes the sales objection — it's free, go verify
- The recursive narrative is inherently viral — people want to share paradoxes
- Targets the skeptic: "Prove it." "We did. On ourselves."

## Risk
- Could feel arrogant without the right tone
- Mitigation: the contemplative music and measured voice make it feel like discovery, not bragging
