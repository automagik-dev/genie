# Brand Angles — All Three, Different Videos

## The Three Lenses

### A) Felipe as Main Character (Personal Brand)
- "12,474 contributions. One human + AI agents."
- Build-in-public, @levelsio/@marc_louvion playbook
- Builds audience that follows the PERSON
- Best for: X, build-in-public community

### B) Genie as Hero (Product Brand)
- "Wishes in, PRs out."
- Product-first, Cursor/Bolt playbook
- The tool speaks for itself
- Best for: Reddit, HN, technical communities

### C) Hybrid (Felipe tells, Genie delivers)
- "I shipped 77 PRs this week. Here's how."
- Personal hook → product reveal
- Highest conversion ceiling
- Best for: LinkedIn, cross-platform

## Video × Brand Matrix

Each video concept works with ANY brand angle. Here's the best fit:

| Video Concept | Best Brand Angle | Alt Angle | Hook Changes |
|---------------|-----------------|-----------|-------------|
| A — The Numbers | **Hybrid (C)** | Product (B) | C: "I merged 77 PRs this week" / B: "77 PRs. 7 days. 96% SHIP rate" |
| B — Overnight Mode | **Personal (A)** | Hybrid (C) | A: "I went to sleep..." / C: "I queued 5 features with Genie..." |
| C — Context Collapse | **Product (B)** | Hybrid (C) | B: show chaos→order / C: add "this was me last month" intro |
| D — It Ships Itself | **Product (B)** | Personal (A) | B: "Genie ships Genie" / A: "I built a tool that ships itself" |
| E — Speed Run | **Hybrid (C)** | Personal (A) | C: timer + pipeline demo / A: "Watch me ship a feature in 42 minutes" |

## Platform × Brand Fit

| Platform | Best Brand Angle | Why |
|----------|-----------------|-----|
| X (Twitter) | Personal (A) or Hybrid (C) | Personal stories stop the scroll. Numbers flex = engagement. |
| Reddit | Product (B) | r/programming wants tools, not personalities. "I built X to solve Y." |
| LinkedIn | Hybrid (C) | Professional narrative. "Here's what I shipped and the tool behind it." |
| Hacker News | Product (B) | "Show HN: Genie — wishes in, PRs out" |
| YouTube/Shorts | Personal (A) | Face + personality + journey = subscribers |

## Decision Framework
Don't decide now. Produce all versions. Let the platforms tell you what resonates.
- Week 1: Post one video per angle (A, B, C) on different platforms
- Week 2: Double down on what got engagement
- Week 3: Iterate on the winning angle

The data decides. Not us.
