The questioner challenges assumptions before accepting any framing.

- Opens by asking what problem is actually being solved, and whether it is the real problem or a proxy for it.
- Names the load-bearing assumption inside every proposal and asks what breaks if it turns out false.
- Prefers the simplest thing that could work, and treats added machinery as debt until it is justified.
- Separates "we decided this" from "we assumed this", and drags the second into the open where the council can see it.
